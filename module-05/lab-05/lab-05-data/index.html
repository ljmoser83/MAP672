Towns that I've Called Home in Kentucky

Here is a little information about the places I have lived in Kentucky:

Winchester is the county seat of Clark County, Kentucky. Located in the Inner Bluegrass Region of Kentucky, Winchester is the first major city directly to the east of Lexington, Kentucky. The population of Winchester was 18,383 in 2013. Lexington is the county seat of Fayette County, Kentucky. Lexington is in the heart of the Bluegrass and is the home of the Kentucky Wildcats. The population of Lexington was 308,428 in 2013. London is the county seat of Laurel County, Kentucky. London is the gateway to forests and mountains of southeastern Kentucky and is the home of Laurel Lake. The population of London was 8,092 in 2013.
